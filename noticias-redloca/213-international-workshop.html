INTERNATIONAL WORKSHOP ON LOCATIONAL ANALYSIS AND RELATED PROBLEMS 2017

VIII International Workshop on Locational Analysis and Related Problems

September 27-29, 2017 -- Segovia (SPAIN)

Topics:

The topics of interest are location analysis and related problems. It includes location models, networks, transportation, logistics, exact and heuristic solution methods, and computational geometry, among others.

Place:

Campus Universitario de Segovia.

Important dates:

Deadline for abstracts (2 pages maximum): May 31

Conference: September 27-29, 2017
